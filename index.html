About

I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at SJTU, starting in 2021 and continuing to the present. My advisors are Prof. Gongshen Liu and Assoc. Prof. Zhuosheng Zhang.

From Oct 2024 to Oct 2025, I have been a visiting student at NUS CTIC under the supervision of Prof. Mong-Li Lee and Prof. Wynne Hsu. I am also mentored by Dr. Hao Fei.

My research interests include Large Language Models, Multi-Agent Systems for Social Simulation, and Interpretability.
